Heteronomy

[ het-uh-ron-uh-mee ]

noun

The condition of being under the domination of an outside authority, either human or divine.

Jurisprudence

Habeas orpus

[ ha-be-as cor-pus ]

noun

A writ requiring a person under arrest to be brought before a judge or into court, especially to secure the person's release unless lawful grounds are shown for their detention.

Suppression

[ suh-presh-uhn ]

noun

1. The act of ending something by force.
2. The act of preventing something from being seen or expressed or from operating.
